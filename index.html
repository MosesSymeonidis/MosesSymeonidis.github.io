About

I am a Senior Researcher and Technical Coordinator at the Laboratory for Internet Computing (LInC) at the University of Cyprus, where I focus on distributed systems, Big Data Analytics, Edge Computing, and the Internet of Things. I hold a PhD in Computer Science (UCY, 2022), with a dissertation on emulating data-intensive IoT applications across the Edge Continuum.


I have been actively involved in cutting-edge research through multiple EU-funded projects (PaaSPort FP7, UNICORN H2020, RAINBOW H2020, TrialsNet, aerOS, 6G Sandbox), securing over €196K in research funding as Proposal Author and Technical Coordinator. My work spans cloud computing, distributed systems, streaming analytics, federated learning, and energy-efficient AI. I also serve as Adjunct Lecturer at the University of Cyprus and the University of Nicosia, teaching Big Data Analytics, Cloud Computing, and Data Science courses at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels.
